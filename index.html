Scrum
A scrum is a method of restarting play after a minor infringement. It involves the forwards from both teams binding together to contest possession of the ball.
Lineout
A lineout occurs when the ball goes into touch (out of bounds). Players from both teams line up and attempt to catch or tap the ball as it is thrown in from the sideline.
Maul
A maul is formed when a player carrying the ball is held by one or more opponents but remains on their feet, and teammates bind together to push forward and retain possession.
Penalty Kick
A penalty kick is awarded for more serious infractions. The team awarded the penalty has the option to kick at goal, kick for touch, or take a quick tap to restart play.
Drop Goal
A drop goal is scored when a player kicks the ball through the goalposts during open play. It is worth 3 points in Rugby Union and Rugby League.
